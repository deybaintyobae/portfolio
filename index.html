Why Nursing?

My father — my greatest inspiration
"

I chose nursing because when I was a little kid, my father was always sick so I got a bit of an experience in the hospital. I was always there by his side — watching, learning, feeling — until I grew up.

My father became my inspiration until today. His strength and the care he received from nurses sparked something deep inside me. Seeing how nurses made a difference in his life made me want to do the same for others.

Dedicated to my father — the reason behind every lesson I learn and every step I take in this journey.
